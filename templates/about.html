About TalentHub Mtaani

TalentHub Mtaani is a digital platform built with the vision of uplifting the youth of Kibra, Nairobi, by connecting their talents to real-world opportunities. From coders and photographers to tailors and musicians, this hub exists to make sure no talent goes unnoticed.

We believe that every young person in Kibra deserves a chance to thrive, and that opportunity should be as close as their community. TalentHub Mtaani serves as a bridge — helping youth build digital portfolios, apply for gigs, learn skills, and find mentors.

This initiative is powered by the community, for the community. Whether you're showcasing your work, looking to grow through learning, or hoping to connect with fellow creatives and professionals, TalentHub Mtaani is your launchpad to success.

Our Mission

To empower Kibra’s youth by providing access to opportunity, skill development, and meaningful mentorship through a simple, community-driven platform.

Our Vision

A thriving community where every talented youth in Kibra has the visibility and tools to succeed — from local recognition to global impact.
