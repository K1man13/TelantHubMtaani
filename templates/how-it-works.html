How TalentHub Mtaani Works

TalentHub Mtaani helps youth in Kibra showcase their talents and connect with opportunities through five simple steps:

Step 1: Create Your Profile

Sign up and build a digital portfolio by uploading photos of your work, describing your projects, and adding certificates. This makes your skills visible in the community.

Step 2: Post or Apply for Gigs

Use the Gigs section to offer or apply for jobs, whether it's paid work, volunteer opportunities, or event-based roles such as performing or designing.

Step 3: Access the Learning Hub

Learn and grow by exploring online courses or joining offline training events around Kibra to upgrade your skills.

Step 4: Join the Mentorship Corner

Connect with experienced mentors for guidance — either virtually or in person — and receive support tailored to your field.

Step 5: Geo-map Talents

Discover talents and gigs near you using a map, allowing for easy networking and collaboration within the Kibra community.

TalentHub Mtaani makes it easy to grow your talent, connect with mentors, and find real gigs — all from your neighborhood.
