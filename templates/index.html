Welcome to TalentHub Mtaani

TalentHub Mtaani is a digital platform designed to uplift the youth of Kibra, Nairobi, by connecting their talents to real-world opportunities. From coders and photographers to tailors and musicians, this hub exists to make sure no talent goes unnoticed.

We believe that every young person in Kibra deserves a chance to thrive, and that opportunity should be as close as their community. TalentHub Mtaani serves as a bridge — helping youth build digital portfolios, apply for gigs, learn skills, and find mentors.
